About me

I am a Full Stack Web Developer who started in Networking!

I have worked in the hospitality industy for most of my professional career. My backgrouund is very diverse from resturaunts and bartending to a youth care worker and insurance agent. My heart truely belongs to technology. I have always had an exposure to it and I love to figure how things work and why they break. This all started when I was very little and began to play video games. I have played cosoles from my original Nintendo to PS4. I have build my own gaming rig and invested in quality hardware stickly as a hobby. But my love for technology no longer stops as my hobby.

After Covid made it's way through the United States and I found myself with more time, it was reinforced that this is was my time to pursue my dreams. I earned my Cisco Certified Network Assosiate (CCNA) certification through CED Solutions. This was my first exposure to code, as they ask you very basic code questiuons. I tried to self teach and ended up finding my way through a few youtube videos, but that wasn't very productive at first. I decided to take advantage of the pandemic and made another investment in myself: I applied and began the UNC Coding Bootcamp.

The UNC Full Stack Flex Development program is a 6 month coding bootcamp based out of the University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill involving hours of instruction through an extensive curriculum using HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, jQuery, Git, Node, Express React, MVC, MySQL, MongoDB, and cloud application development. I graduated September 11, 2021 and was encouraged by my instruction staff to apply as a Teaching Assistant. In October of 2021, I began helping students who were in the same position I was! As of right now, I am in pursuit of a full time position that allows me to work with the hardware that gives me joy day in and day out.
